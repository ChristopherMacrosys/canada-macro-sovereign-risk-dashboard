# Canada Macroeconomic & Sovereign Risk Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a macroeconomic and sovereign-risk monitoring framework focused on Canada’s evolving macro-financial environment. The dashboard combines macroeconomic indicators, commodity-price dynamics, FX analysis, and structural commentary to assess medium-term sovereign and economic risks.

The framework integrates publicly available macro-financial data with institutional-style analysis focused on growth momentum, inflation dynamics, productivity constraints, investment conditions, and external vulnerabilities.

---

## Key Themes

- Inflation and monetary-policy conditions
- Household debt sensitivity and domestic demand pressure
- Oil-price and geopolitical transmission effects
- USD/CAD and external macro signals
- Structural productivity and investment challenges
- Housing, labour mobility, and internal market efficiency
- Long-term sovereign resilience and competitiveness

---

## Tools & Methodology

- Python

- Bank of Canada Data

---

## Structural Commentary

The report incorporates institutional macroeconomic commentary informed by:
- Bank of Canada publications
- OECD Canada Economic Outlook
- Global Risk Institute economic commentary

The framework is designed to identify directional changes in macro-financial and sovereign-risk conditions rather than generate deterministic forecasts.
